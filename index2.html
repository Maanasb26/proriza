Proriza Products Development Process

Mutation Breeding

It's a conventional breeding method highly suitable to enhance the nutritional value of crops. We had screened thousands of mutant plants of rice to discover plants capable of expressing high resistant starch, dietary fibre and protein contents.

Hybridization

We utilised the classical hybridisation methods to stack a number of mutations in a common high yielding varietal backgrounds so that all the enhanced nutritional parameters are expressed without yield penalty.

Selection

We use modern biochemical screening methods to select mutants and segregants from our hybridisation programmes to end up with breeding materials with multiple health benefits.

Pledge

Proriza Rice products are not a Genetic Modified Organism (GMO) as we never employ genetic engineering techniques or gene transfer methods.
